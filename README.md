# Form Designer

A simple **drag-and-drop** form builder that allows users to add, reorder, and remove form components. The form is saved in **local storage**, enabling users to retain their form structure even after refreshing the page.

## 🚀 Features
- 🏗 **Drag & Drop** - Reorder form elements with a smooth drag-and-drop interface.
- 🎨 **Add Form Fields** - Input, Select, Textarea, and Checkbox components.
- 🗑 **Delete Components** - Remove unwanted fields with a simple button click.
- 💾 **Local Storage** - Form data is automatically saved and restored.
- 🖥 **Responsive Design** - Works well on desktops and mobile devices.

## 📌 How to Use
1. **Click on a button** in the sidebar to add a new form component.
2. **Drag & drop components** to rearrange their order.
3. **Click the delete button** (🗑️) to remove a component.
4. **Click "Save"** to confirm the form (data is stored locally).
